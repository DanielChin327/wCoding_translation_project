What is the Al Bhed Language?

The Al Bhed language is a constructed language used by the Al Bhed tribe in the video game "Final Fantasy X." It is essentially a substitution cipher of the English language, where each letter in the English alphabet is replaced with a corresponding letter in the Al Bhed alphabet. This makes it relatively straightforward to translate between English and Al Bhed, once you have the substitution key.

Key Features of Al Bhed

  • Alphabet Substitution: Each letter in the English alphabet is replaced with a different letter to form the Al Bhed alphabet.
  • Simple Cipher: It's a one-to-one correspondence, meaning each letter in English maps directly to one letter in Al Bhed and vice versa.
  • In-Game Learning: In "Final Fantasy X," players can find and collect Al Bhed primers, which gradually reveal the cipher key and help the player understand the language.

How to translate

  • Find the Al Bhed Cipher Key: Use the substitution chart or Al Bhed primers found in the game to get the full list of letter mappings.
  • Replace Letters: Substitute each letter in the English text with the corresponding Al Bhed letter using the cipher key.
  • Construct the Sentence: Combine the substituted letters to form the translated text.
